# Simple Recipes

## Overnight Oats
Mix 1/2 cup oats, 1/2 cup milk, and 1/4 cup yogurt. Add a pinch of salt and honey. Chill overnight.

## Tomato Pasta
Saute garlic in olive oil, add crushed tomatoes, simmer 15 minutes. Toss with cooked pasta and basil.

## Quick Salad
Combine greens, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and feta. Dress with olive oil and lemon.
